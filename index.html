About

"I Hack This" is a student team from University of Technology Sarawak. We are passionate about innovation and trying new things. Through every event we participate in, we gain valuable innovation skills and continue growing. We're always looking forward to future interesting events that challenge us to push boundaries and learn more.

Our Core Competencies

  • Application & Website Development
  • Hardware Prototyping & Fabrication
  • Problem-Solving & Innovation
  • Frontend Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Game Development & Interactive Design
